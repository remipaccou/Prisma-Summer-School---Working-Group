**Through-line.** The naive 32% is not a probability (a conditional-forecast count, unstable to its
definition). The ensemble is biased low and beaten by trivial rules; the world is in *addition* mode
(renewables boom, fossils persist); credibility filtering yields no single number; and the cost view
relocates the barrier from technology to substitution.
