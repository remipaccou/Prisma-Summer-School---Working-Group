## Our key messages (reminder)

1. The ensemble missed 2025: reality exceeded most projections
2. Net-zero scenarios systematically underestimate emissions
3. A straight line beats the ensemble on 4 out of 6 variables
4. Coal and solar separate NZ from non-NZ — in opposite directions
5. PV reaches ~$40/kW by 2070 whether the world reaches net zero or not

## One-sentence conclusion

The door to net zero by 2070 is opened by cheap clean technology (falling faster than models assumed) and held shut by fossil inertia (coal is not declining). The barrier is substitution, not cost.
